Sixty Days Six App Shipments Challenge

Sixty days of summer. Sixty days of no college, no assignments, no studies. Sixty Days of learning and coding. What more can one ask for? :)

Since I am not doing any internship or any summer course, I have sixty days with me, and I can do anything I want to in these two months. Surely, not going to be lazying around. I won’t get this huge amount of free time again. This is really the time to do something worthwhile. 

I have an idea, sixty days, six app shipments challenge. Yes, not just app development, but app shipment, too. This means, I will have 10 days for each app, which should be enough time, for a small project. 

Starting from 1st June, I will be writing one post everyday for sixty days, and will be posting about what I learnt that day, how much I moved ahead with the app, basically about the status of apps I will be working on.

Why write a blog post everyday? Sixty days, working on your own, you surely need some motivation. Writing a post everyday, will be an incentive to carry on with this challenge. 

I am so excited about this huge summer project. Will be great to work with more people on this, so that I could finish apps as soon as possible. 

So, who’s in? 

Maybe, I am being a little too optimistic here with six apps is sixty days, but, hey, I have nothing to lose, only to gain :)
